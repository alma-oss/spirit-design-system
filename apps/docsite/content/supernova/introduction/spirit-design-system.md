## Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a **comprehensive design system** that can swiftly **adapt to any business or technological demands**, thereby preventing the redundancy of steps when establishing new frontends. Additionally, Spirit is committed to fostering the development of **accessible websites**, ensuring they are **usable by everyone** without undue barriers.

Some examples of products that are using Spirit:

- Jobboard Platform

- [Jobs.cz](https://jds.supernova-docs.io/latest)

- [Seduo](https://design-system.seduo.com/)

- [Teamio](https://gui.teamio.com/storybook/?path=/docs/teamio-ui-components_components-alert--docs)

- Atmoskop

- Prace za Rohem

For more information, see [What is Spirit](/latest/introduction/what-is-spirit-PDPDSQC7) or message us on Slack in the [#spirit-design-system_cs_en](https://almamedia.slack.com/archives/C050YPZ3YRW) channel.
