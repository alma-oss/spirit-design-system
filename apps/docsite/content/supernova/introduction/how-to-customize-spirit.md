Are you new with Spirit or want to ask anything? Contact us on Slack in the [#spirit-design-system_cs_en](https://almamedia.slack.com/archives/C050YPZ3YRW) channel. There isn't a wrong question :)

### Design setup

![](https://studio-assets.supernova.io/design-systems/10180/e3486e40-c36a-4e8d-b008-984ab6b24541.jpg)

Duplicate the Spirit Figma file. Then change the values of the styles (keep names without changes) and show it to your dev-mate. Here is the list of customizable styles:

- Colors Customization

- [Radii Customization](/latest/design/global-tokens/radius/customisation-18qHkI38)

- [Headings Customization](/latest/design/global-tokens/typography/customisation-IhdVUQ93)

- [Shadows Customization](/latest/design/global-tokens/elevation-and-shadows/brand-customisation-uA6m7MUs)

- Icon Customization

Other styles can be customized unfortunately only at your own risk due to the possibility of breaking some components.
