### For designers

- All shadows are customizable

- Change shadow styles in Figma

- Keep current number of shadows because they are used in components. If you need add more, contact our team on slack [#spirit-design-system_cs_en](https://almamedia.slack.com/archives/C050YPZ3YRW)

![](https://studio-assets.supernova.io/design-systems/10180/6d3d1a89-213c-41c1-ab42-0cbd8729e01e.png)

Are you interested in how to set up the entire brand? Check out [the guide](/latest/introduction/how-to-customize-spirit-BnwsYmmD)

### For developers

- Use only Shadow tokens which are shown in Figma (or Supernova if you use it)

- If you need more shadows or any changes, contact your designer
