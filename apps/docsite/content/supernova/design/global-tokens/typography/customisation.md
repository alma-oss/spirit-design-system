### For designers

- All headings are customizable, so you can use any font family. The best choices are from [Google fonts](https://fonts.google.com/knowledge) because of high quality and easy sharing

- Change texts styles for headings in Figma

- Body texts aren't customizable due to their connection with component sizes

![](https://studio-assets.supernova.io/design-systems/10180/0da484f6-c0f5-4cd1-9642-69e73d626478.png)

Are you interested in how to set up the entire brand? Check out [the guide](/latest/introduction/how-to-customize-spirit-BnwsYmmD)

### For developers

- Use only typography tokens which are shown in Figma (or Supernova if you use it)

- If you need more colors or any changes, contact your designer or us in the [#spirit-design-system_cs_en](https://almamedia.slack.com/archives/C050YPZ3YRW) channel
