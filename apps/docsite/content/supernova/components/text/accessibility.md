## **Accessibility**

The majority of accessibility issues are already guaranteed by implementation. In the case of modifications or composition in HTML, please follow the recommendations below.

### **Semantics**

- Text does not add meaning on its own – select the proper HTML tag for document structure.

### **Contrast**

- Ensure all text meets **WCAG AA** contrast requirements in both light and dark modes.

### **Font Scaling**

- Verify text remains legible when users adjust font size or zoom (up to 200 %).

### **Readability**

- Maintain adequate line height and spacing; avoid tightly packed lines or overly small text.

### **Language**

- Use plain, clear language; avoid stylistic text (like all caps) unless semantically appropriate.
