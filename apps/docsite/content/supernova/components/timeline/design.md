Timeline vertically organizes a series of events, steps, or milestones in chronological order. It supports multiple marker types and flexible content layouts. It is composed of a main container (Timeline) with steps (TimelineStep) that include a marker (TimelineMarker), optional heading (TimelineHeading), and optional content (TimelineContent). Timeline uses a grid-based layout to align markers and content consistently. Connector lines are rendered with pseudo-elements for clean, semantic markup. Headings are optional but recommended for clarity, especially when describing distinct phases or milestones.
