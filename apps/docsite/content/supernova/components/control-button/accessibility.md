## Accessibility

The majority of accessibility issues are already guaranteed by implementation. In the case of modifications or composition in HTML, please follow the recommendations below.

### **Labeling**

- Always provide an **accessible label** when the button has no visible text.

- Labels must clearly describe the action (e.g., “Next slide”, “Scroll down”).

### **Focus and Keyboard Navigation**

- Must be fully keyboard-focusable (Tab) and operable with Enter or Space.

- Visible focus outline should meet Spirit’s standard focus ring contrast.

### **Contrast**

- Ensure sufficient contrast between icon/button color and background in both themes.

- Hover and focus states must remain perceivable without relying solely on color.
